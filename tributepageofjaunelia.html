Jaun Elia

Great pakistani urdu poet and also lyrics writer.

About the Legend

☛ Jaun Elia full name Syed Hussain Jaun Asghar Naqve was born in 14 december 1931 in Amroha,british india.

☛ He was the youngest of his siblings.

☛ He began writing poetry at the age of 8 but published his first collection at the age of 60.

☛ He was a communist so he decided to live in pakistan.

☛ His poetry is famous for love and the unending pain of separation.

☛ He married with writer zahida hina in 1970 and separated in 1992.

☛ His famous collection:
☛ Sukhan Meri Udasee Hai
امید ☛ Mubada
☛ Tumharey Aur Mere Darmiyan
